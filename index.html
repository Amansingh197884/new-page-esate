History of our creation

Derived from the Sanskrit word 'Aavarthan,' meaning 'revolution'. As an architecture and interior design company in Delhi NCR, we have picked this name to reflect our ideology of coming up with new and fresh designs for our clients that are practical, cost-effective, and also offer timeless elegance.

Every space we design is about bringing your dream to life. And we create spaces that not only inspire but also endure. We bring about timeless elegance to balance innovation with tradition.

Why Choose Aavarthan Studio?

At Aavarthan Studio, we turn your vision into reality through a collaborative approach. We take time to understand what needs are, what is your purpose of initiating the architectural and interior work project, and what you have in mind. Then, we come up with some artistic and practical designs and show them to you.

Once you finalise the design you like, we start working on it. Premium materials and expert artisans are the backbone of our architecture, designing and interior work services.

We have established ourselves as the best architecture and interior company in Delhi NCR by creating bespoke interior design solutions and architectural designs tailored to the preferred style of our clients and making sure that their spaces are both unique and purposeful.
